## Summary

In this module, you've learned about the meaning and significance of the concepts of accessibility, disability, and inclusion. Here are some of the things we covered:

- Why accessibility is important for people of all abilities
- How to speak to and interact with a person respectfully regardless of whether or not you know they have a disability
- Person-first language as a recommended best practice that puts the person before their disability
- Examples of how people of all abilities access and interact with technology
