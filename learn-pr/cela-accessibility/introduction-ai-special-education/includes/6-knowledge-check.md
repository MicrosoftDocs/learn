Test your understanding of AI in special education with these key questions:

## Knowledge Check
**1. Which best describes the role of AI in special education?**  
A. AI replaces teacher decision‑making  
B. AI supports teachers by streamlining tasks and creating inclusive learning environments  
C. AI eliminates the need for accessibility tools  
**Correct Answer:** B

**2. What is one example of how AI can help teachers communicate with families?**  
A. Drafting multilingual caregiver updates using Microsoft 365 Copilot and Translator  
B. Automatically scheduling parent‑teacher conferences without teacher input  
C. Sending progress reports without review  
**Correct Answer:** A

**3. Which is a key challenge of using AI in education?**  
A. AI tools are too slow to be useful  
B. Risk of bias, inaccuracies, and privacy violations if outputs are not reviewed  
C. AI cannot generate differentiated materials  
**Correct Answer:** B

**4. Which principle is NOT part of Microsoft’s Responsible AI framework?**  
A. Fairness  
B. Transparency  
C. Automation  
**Correct Answer:** C

**5. When drafting prompts for AI tools, what is the best practice to protect student privacy?**  
A. Include full student names and detailed personal data  
B. Use general descriptors and avoid personally identifiable information  
C. Share prompts publicly for collaboration  
**Correct Answer:** B
