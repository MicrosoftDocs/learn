Meetings play an important role in helping teams collaborate, make decisions, and coordinate work. However, capturing important information while actively participating in a discussion can be challenging.

In this module, you learned how Facilitator in Microsoft Teams helps support meeting collaboration by generating collaborative notes, helping capture decisions, and helping document action items during meetings.

You explored how Facilitator can help participants stay focused on the conversation while maintaining visibility into meeting outcomes. You also learned how to use Facilitator during a Teams meeting and how to review generated notes, decisions, and action items after the meeting concludes.

Key takeaways include:

- Facilitator is an AI-powered meeting assistant in Microsoft Teams.
- Facilitator helps create collaborative notes that participants can review and contribute to during meetings.
- Facilitator can help capture decisions and action items as discussions occur.
- Reviewing meeting outcomes helps ensure notes, decisions, and action items remain accurate and actionable.
- Shared meeting content can help teams stay aligned before, during, and after meetings.

By using Facilitator to organize meeting information and support collaboration, teams can maintain a clearer record of discussions, decisions, and next steps.
