Meetings are where teams share ideas, make decisions, and identify action items. But keeping everyone aligned can be challenging when participants are focused on contributing to the discussion while also trying to capture notes, decisions, and action items.

When important details are missed or follow-up tasks aren't clearly documented, teams can spend additional time revisiting conversations, clarifying responsibilities, or searching for information after the meeting ends.

Facilitator in Microsoft Teams helps address these challenges by serving as an AI-powered meeting assistant. Facilitator can generate collaborative notes in real time, help capture decisions, help identify action items, and help participants stay focused on the discussion instead of manual note taking.

In this module, you learn how Facilitator supports collaboration during Microsoft Teams meetings, explore its key capabilities, and discover how to use it to help keep meetings organized and actionable.

## Learning objectives

By the end of this module, you're able to:

- Describe the purpose of Facilitator in Microsoft Teams.
- Identify key Facilitator capabilities that support meeting collaboration.
- Use Facilitator during Microsoft Teams meetings.
- Help review and collaborate on AI-generated meeting notes, decisions, and action items.
