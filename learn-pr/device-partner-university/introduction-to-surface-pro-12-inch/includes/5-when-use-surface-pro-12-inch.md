Surface Pro 12-inch offers flexibility, manageability, and performance for modern business users. While many types of users can see value from this device, it's ideal for frontline workers and those in the education sector (specifically those in higher education).

- **Frontline Workers:** Imagine you're a hotel manager, often needing to view employee requests while ensuring that your hotel is running optimally. Performance, portability, and battery life are essential to get your work done. Surface Pro 12-inch meets these requirements, offering AI-powered communication tools like improved Windows Search to find files quickly. Users also benefit from various interaction methods, allowing them to use Surface Pro 12-inch that suits their environment the best.
- **Education:** Imagine you're a student looking to balance your course load between school and home. You need a laptop that provides reliable performance and lasts your entire day. With the Qualcomm Snapdragon X Plus (8-core) X1P-42-100 processor and dedicated Neural Processing Unit, Surface Pro 12-inch has the power to organize your assignments and notes, with build quality that supports frequent use and a battery that supports up to 16 hours of battery life.1

- **IT decision makers:** When IT departments are looking for new devices for their teams, they focus on easy deployment and management. Whether in scenarios across a distributed workforce or in the hands of students and faculty, Surface Pro 12-inch helps users remain flexible and accomplish their tasks efficiently.
