As a partner using Surface Support Portal, you can do the following:

- Create Service Orders.
- Create Support Requests.
- Track service and support request status.
- Monitor request lifecycle indicators.

You should escalate when:

- You can't access the correct tenant.
- Required Surface Support Portal roles are missing.
- Tenant ownership or authorization is unclear.
- A request appears under the wrong tenant context.

Escalation typically involves:

- Contacting the customer's Global Administrator.
- Requesting role assignment or tenant validation.
