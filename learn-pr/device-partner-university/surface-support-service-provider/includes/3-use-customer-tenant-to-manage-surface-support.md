In most scenarios, partners manage Surface support by accessing the customer's Microsoft tenant in Surface Support Portal. In this tenant-based support model:

- The customer already has a Microsoft tenant.
- The partner is granted the appropriate access role in Surface Support Portal in the customer tenant.
- Service Orders and Support Requests are created and tracked on behalf of the customer.

This approach confirms that:

- Requests are associated with the customer's tenant.
- Service history and tracking remain visible within the customer environment.
- Lifecycle tracking remains centralized in Surface Support Portal.

**Scenario:** You support a customer who already has a Microsoft tenant and needs help submitting Surface repair requests.

**Correct action:** Request Surface Support Portal access in the customer's tenant, rather than creating a new tenant or using your own.
