In some limited scenarios, a partner may use their own tenant to manage Surface service and support in Surface Support Portal. This typically applies when:

- The partner manages service logistics centrally.
- The partner submits and tracks Service Orders or Support Requests across multiple customer organizations.

Important considerations for these scenarios include:

- Requests are submitted and tracked within the partner's tenant.
- Device information must be entered manually (such as serial numbers).
- Customers may not have direct visibility into service tracking unless processes are defined separately.
- The partner assumes responsibility for monitoring lifecycle status in Surface Support Portal.

**Scenario:** A partner manages device repairs for multiple customer tenants and submits service orders on their behalf.

**Correct action:** Sign into the Surface Support Portal using the partner tenant, then submit and track service requests in line with the partner's internal governance processes.
