Partners who manage multiple customer tenants must ensure they're operating in the correct tenant context before creating or tracking any service or support request. Because Surface Support Portal is tenant-scoped, submitting a request in the wrong tenant may:

- Associate service records with the incorrect organization.
- Cause tracking discrepancies.
- Require corrective escalation.

Best practices include:

- Confirming the tenant name before creating requests.
- Verifying the customer context matches the device being serviced.
- Reviewing tenant indicators before submitting shipping information.
- Avoiding simultaneous sessions across multiple tenants when possible.

**Scenario:** You're logged into Surface Support Portal and preparing to submit a service order and you support multiple customers.

**Correct action:** Confirm that the tenant name and customer context before entering device or shipping details.
