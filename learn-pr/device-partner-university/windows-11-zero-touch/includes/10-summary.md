In this module, you worked through a complete Windows 11 Pro deployment scenario from start to finish.

You learned how to:

- Analyze deployment constraints and determine when a zero-touch approach is required
- Prepare devices for zero-touch provisioning and automatic enrollment
- Verify that devices are fully managed, configured, and ready for use
- Define update behavior that keeps devices secure while minimizing disruption
- Apply and validate baseline security controls across the device lifecycle
- Validate deployment readiness and troubleshoot issues using management and compliance signals

You can now apply this approach to your own Windows 11 Pro deployments, adapting it to your organization's requirements while maintaining consistency, security, and scalability.
