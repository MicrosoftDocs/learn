This module shows how to access Surface Management Portal through the Microsoft Intune admin center, verify prerequisites, assign required roles, and confirm that your environment is ready to manage Surface devices.

## Learning objectives:

By the end of this module, you'll be able to:

- Access the Microsoft Intune Admin Center and locate the Surface Management Portal.
- Verify that your organization meets the prerequisites for using Surface Management Portal, including Intune licensing, Surface device enrollment, and required role eligibility.
- Confirm that Surface devices are properly enrolled in Intune and eligible for management through Surface Management Portal.
- Validate successful access to Surface Management Portal by confirming portal visibility and reviewing device health and inventory data.

Use this module when you need to confirm that your organization and Surface devices are ready for management in Surface Management Portal.
