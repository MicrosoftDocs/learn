Before using Surface Management Portal, it's important to verify that a few requirements are met. Confirm the following:

✅Confirm your organization has an Intune E3 or E5 subscription available.

✅Verify that your organization's Microsoft Surface devices are already enrolled in Intune.

✅Authenticate that you're registered as eligible for required admin roles.

It's important to note that if devices are deployed but not enrolled in Intune, Surface Management Portal can't be used.

>[!IMPORTANT]
> To access Surface Management Portal, your organization must have an active Intune E3 or E5 subscription and the managed Surface devices must be enrolled in Intune.
