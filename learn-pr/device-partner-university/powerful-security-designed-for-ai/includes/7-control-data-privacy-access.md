As organizations adopt AI across devices and cloud services, they need a consistent approach to management and governance.

Copilot+ PCs, together with Microsoft 365 and Microsoft Intune, enable organizations to apply the same tools, policies, and security frameworks used for devices and applications to AI experiences.

This approach allows organizations to manage how AI features are configured, how data is protected, and how access is controlled—using familiar tools and processes already in place.

By applying consistent management and governance across both on-device and cloud-based AI, organizations can help ensure that AI-powered capabilities remain secure, compliant, and aligned with organizational requirements as they're introduced and scaled.
