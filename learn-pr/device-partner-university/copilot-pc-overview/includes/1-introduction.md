Copilot+ PCs represent the most advanced class of Windows devices—purpose-built for AI. With a supercharged Neural Processing Unit (NPU), deep integration with Windows 11 Pro, and the ability to run AI workloads locally, these devices serve as the foundation for a new era of intelligent, secure computing.

In this module, we take a holistic look at Copilot+ PCs—exploring their unique combination of hardware and software advancements. You learn how these devices deliver real business value and respond to the growing need for AI-powered performance, security, and productivity in today’s workplace.

## Learning objectives

By the end of this module, you are able to:

- Define key terms such as CPU, GPU, NPU, and TOPS
- Describe how Copilot+ PCs support productivity, performance, and security
- Understand the significance of the NPU and its effect on efficiency
- Explain how Copilot+ PCs support AI agents and on-device AI workloads
- Describe the role of Windows as a managed platform for AI
