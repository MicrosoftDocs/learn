Microsoft Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) is a Copilot+ PC built by Microsoft that is designed specifically for business users. Combining software, hardware, AI features, and multiple connectivity options, Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) allows business users to complete routine tasks quickly—enabling them to achieve their goals with the latest hardware and software advancements.

## Hardware

Imagine that you're a business user that needs a device that balances performance, portability, and connectivity to get your work done. Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) offers hardware that fits these needs. Let's look at some of the device specifications that solve this need without added complexity:

- **Powerful processors:** Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) features the Intel® Core™ Ultra Series 3 processors, a silicon platform that delivers premium performance, powers AI experiences, and offers improved graphics over previous generations. This generation of processors features the latest performance, efficiency, and low-power efficiency cores to offer a balance of powerful performance and long battery life. These processors feature a dedicated Neural Processing Unit (NPU) capable of  50 Trillion of Operation Per Second (TOPS)—enabling key Copilot+ PC AI experiences that run locally on device. Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) features the following processors:
  - Intel® Core™ Ultra 5 processor 335 (Series 3)
  - Intel® Core™ Ultra 7 processor 366H (Series 3)
  - Intel® Core™ Ultra X7 processor 368H (Series 3)
- **Displays:** Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) features two different screen size configurations: 13.8" and 15". Both displays feature a PixelSense™ Flow touchscreen display with a dynamic refresh rate of 120Hz and 10-point multitouch. The displays are individually color-calibrated with adaptive color and contrast—enabling contrast and clarity in various lighting conditions. Both displays feature a 3:2 aspect ratio, ideal for scrolling vertical content in Microsoft Office, and an anti-reflective display that is ISO 9241-307 certified. The 13.8" display option also features anti-glare technology with an integrated privacy screen—excellent for keeping data secure in various working environments. - **Ports and charging:** Both size configurations of Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) feature two USB-C/Thunderbolt™ 4 ports with support for charging, data transfer, DisplayPort 2.1, and Surface Thunderbolt™ Dock. They also feature one USB-A 3.2 port, a 3.5-mm headphone port, and Surface Connect Port. The devices support fast charging with a minimum of 60W power supply via the Surface Connect Port or USB-C. This port selection enables business users to have the versatility of different workstation setups and utilize essential accessories for their workflow.

>[!NOTE]
> The Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) 15" model also features a MicroSDXC Express card reader for removable storage and an optional integrated smart card reader, helping protect business users against sophisticated cyber threats.

- **Precision touchpad:** The touchpad on Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) is customizable with Adaptive Touch mode and advanced haptics that provide intuitive navigation and responsive feedback, such as feeling an image click into place in PowerPoint. Adaptive Touch allows users with varying mobility and dexterity levels to customize the touchpad to better suit their needs.

To enable Adaptive Touch, follow these steps:

1. Open the **Surface app**.
2. Select **Touchpad settings**.
3. Enable Adaptive Touch by toggling the switch to **On** and then select **Turn on**.

## Software

In our scenario, you're a business user that places a focus on performance. On-device Copilot+ PC AI features on Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) help to accomplish their tasks.

- **Click to Do:** This feature identifies text and images on your screen, analyzes them, and provides actionable options that you can select based on the content. Click to Do identifies only the presence of text and images—not their actual content—and performs all processing locally on the device.
- **Agent in Settings:** Using on-device AI in Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition), Agent in Settings helps users find and change settings on their Surface. It can also help troubleshoot issues by providing recommendations and automating tasks based on input. The agent guides users through finding and changing settings, offering relevant information and recommendations. By using natural language queries and utilizing an AI model for intelligent search suggestions, this feature enhances search within Windows Settings.
- **Custom AI workflows:** Foundry on Windows, Copilot Studio, and Copilot Agents enable organizations to design and extend AI-powered workflows that align with their business requirements. These tools support customization by allowing teams to connect AI capabilities with existing applications, data, and processes—ensuring AI solutions fit established workflows rather than requiring users to adapt how they work. This approach makes it easier to integrate AI into everyday tasks while maintaining control and consistency across the organization.

## Security

Security is a top priority for IT teams and small business owners. Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) delivers security and control for peace of mind, fortified with trusted Microsoft protection at every layer.

- **Integrated TPM (Trusted Platform Module) 2.0:** Helps to protect sensitive information and enables Windows security features like BitLocker, Windows Hello for Business, and Windows Defender System Guard. Microsoft Pluton provides a platform for future security updates. TPM 2.0 also enables a fast, secure device with app logins with a built-in facial recognition scanner, Windows Hello for Business, and Enhanced Sign-In Security (ESS).
- **Secured-core PC:** Windows integrates closely with Surface hardware to deliver protections that take advantage of hardware capabilities. As a Secured-core PC, Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) uses TPM 2.0 for hardware root of trust, secure boot, and BitLocker drive encryption. This hardware provides protection against malware, hardware threats, and unauthorized access.
- **Device Firmware Configuration Interface (DFCI):** Designed to be used with software-level mobile device management (MDM), DFCI enables IT admins to remotely disable specific hardware components and prevent end users from accessing them. For example, if you need to protect sensitive information in highly secure areas, you can disable the camera, and if you don't want users booting from USB drives, you can disable that as well.
- **Data transfer and repair:** Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) offers removable internal storage that simplifies data retention for device transfer and decommissioning. A QR code is also included, linking to the device's serial number to simplify tracking and management. On-site repairs for serviceable components are made easier thanks to wayfinding icons and replaceable components.
- **Windows Autopilot:** Deploying Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) is simplified for IT teams with Windows Autopilot. This feature simplifies the Windows device lifecycle, for both IT and end users, from initial deployment to end of life. Autopilot streamlines IT deployment processes, minimizes infrastructure maintenance requirements, and offers usability for end users.
