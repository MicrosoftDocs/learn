Microsoft Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) is a Copilot+ PC designed for business users, combining modern hardware, on-device AI capabilities, and built-in security to support productivity across a range of work environments.

With Intel® Core™ Ultra processors, adaptable display options, and versatile connectivity, Surface Laptop for Business (8th Edition) enables users to balance performance, portability, and flexibility while completing everyday tasks efficiently.

On-device AI features—such as Click to Do and Agent in Settings—help enhance workflows by enabling faster access to information, simplifying configuration tasks, and supporting intelligent actions directly on the device.

In addition, enterprise-grade security, deployment tools like Windows Autopilot, and manageability features such as DFCI provide IT teams with control and protection across the device lifecycle.
