Organizations managing Surface devices use two primary portals: Surface Management Portal and Surface Support Portal.

These portals support different tasks in the Surface device lifecycle. Understanding when to use each portal helps administrators manage devices efficiently and initiate support or service actions correctly.

## Learning objectives

- Identify the purpose and core functions of the Surface Management Portal and Surface Support Portal.
- Compare the capabilities of Surface Management Portal and Surface Support Portal and determine which portal to use for common management and support scenarios based on Intune enrollment and task type.
