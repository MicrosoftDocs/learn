Organizations are adopting AI to improve productivity, streamline workflows, and enable new ways of working. Copilot+ PCs are a category of Windows 11 devices that bring AI capabilities into everyday work through a combination of on-device processing and cloud-connected services.

In this module, you explore how Copilot+ PCs support business outcomes by combining on-device AI, cloud services, and Windows 11 Pro capabilities to improve productivity, flexibility, security, and IT management across modern work environments.

> [!NOTE]
> This module is designed for learners who want to understand how Copilot+ PCs support modern organizations, including business decision makers, IT professionals, and partner sellers evaluating AI-enabled Windows devices.

## Learning objectives

By the end of this module, you're able to:

- Explain how Copilot+ PCs support modern business workflows  
- Describe how on-device AI and cloud services work together in Windows  
- Identify key areas where Copilot+ PCs improve productivity, security, and efficiency  
- Summarize how Copilot+ PCs support AI-driven workflows in organizations
