Modern organizations require devices that can support flexible workstyles, hybrid collaboration, and AI-powered productivity across different locations and connectivity conditions.

Copilot+ PCs help support these environments by combining on-device AI processing, cloud-connected services, and Windows 11 Pro management capabilities into a unified experience.

## Enabling flexible work

With AI processing available directly on the device through the NPU, users can continue working even when connectivity is limited.

Features like Live Captions, Click to Do, and Improved Windows Search run locally, helping provide a consistent experience whether employees are in the office, working remotely, or traveling.

This flexibility helps organizations support hybrid work environments where employees move between locations throughout the day without disrupting workflows.

## Supporting responsive AI experiences

On-device AI processing helps reduce delays associated with cloud-only processing and allows supported experiences to respond in real time.

Organizations can benefit from:

- Faster responsiveness for supported AI experiences
- Reduced reliance on constant internet connectivity
- Improved multitasking during AI-assisted workflows
- More consistent experiences across different work environments

These capabilities are especially valuable for collaboration, communication, and productivity scenarios where responsiveness and reliability are important.

## Improving efficiency and mobility

NPUs are designed to process AI workloads more efficiently than traditional processors alone, helping balance AI performance with power consumption.

For organizations, this can help support:

- Longer battery life during AI-assisted workflows
- Reduced energy consumption for AI processing
- Improved mobility for employees working across locations
- Consistent performance throughout the workday

These capabilities help organizations support modern work environments while maintaining flexibility, productivity, and operational efficiency.

## Supporting modern collaboration

Copilot+ PCs also help improve collaboration experiences through AI-enhanced communication features built into Windows 11.

Examples include:

- **Live Captions**—Provides real-time translation and captioning across audio sources
- **Windows Studio Effects**—Enhances video and audio quality during meetings
- **Voice Focus**—Helps reduce background noise during calls and collaboration sessions

Together, these capabilities help organizations support more accessible, flexible, and responsive communication across distributed teams.
