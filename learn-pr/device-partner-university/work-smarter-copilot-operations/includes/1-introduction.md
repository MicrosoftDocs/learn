Copilot+ PCs bring business-ready AI to every department—unlocking new efficiencies and delivering better outcomes with AI-powered features and solutions designed for real-world results. In this module, you'll explore how the combination of Copilot+ PC and Microsoft 365 Copilot (sold separately) helps turn everyday Operations moments like incident escalation or weekly reporting into smoother, more productive workflows.

## Learning objectives

By the end of this module, you'll be able to:

- Use Copilot in Outlook and Teams to summarize communications, identify action items, and support daily operational workflows.
- Apply Copilot+ PC features such as Click to Do, Improved Windows Search, and Live Captions to streamline coordination and task completion.
- Use collaboration tools such as Windows Studio Effects (Studio Effects) and Copilot in Teams to improve stakeholder communication, meeting follow-up, and alignment.
- Prepare for the next workday by using Copilot and device features to create recaps, schedule focused work, and reset settings efficiently.
