In this module, you explored how operations professionals can use Microsoft 365 Copilot and Copilot+ PC features to improve communication, coordination, and end-of-day planning. From summarizing email and chat activity to capturing meeting follow-up, finding files faster, and preparing for the next workday, these tools help reduce manual effort and keep work moving efficiently.

## Key takeaways

- Use Copilot in Outlook and Teams to summarize communications, identify action items, and support daily operational workflows.
- Use Click to Do and Improved Windows Search to quickly schedule meetings and find files with natural language.
- Use Live Captions and Windows Studio Effects to improve communication and collaboration with stakeholders.
- Use Copilot and Agent in Settings at the end of the day to create recaps, schedule focused work, and reset device settings for tomorrow.
