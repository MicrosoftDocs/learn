Create a service order when a Surface device requires:

- Physical repair
- Hardware replacement
- Exchange due to device failure

To get started, you must first determine the correct starting portal.

## Step 1: Determine the correct starting portal

Where you start to create a service order depends on device enrollment:

- **Surface Management Portal** ➡️ Use if your organization has an Intune subscription. You can create service orders for both enrolled and unenrolled Surface devices directly from Surface Management Portal.
- **Surface Support Portal** ➡️ Use if your organization doesn't have an Intune license. Surface Support Portal is required to create service orders when Intune isn't available.

If starting in Surface Management Portal:

- In the Microsoft Intune Admin Center, navigate to **Devices > All devices**.
- Select the enrolled Surface device.
- Open its **Device details** page.
- Initiate the service action directly from the enrolled device's details in Intune.

If starting in Surface Support Portal:

- Navigate directly to service order creation.
- Enter device details manually.

## Step 2: Provide required information

When prompted for required information, you'll need to provide the following:

- Device serial number
- Description of the issue
- Shipping and contact details

>[!IMPORTANT]
> Regardless of which Surface portal a request is initiated in, it can be tracked in either Surface Support Portal or Surface Management Portal.
