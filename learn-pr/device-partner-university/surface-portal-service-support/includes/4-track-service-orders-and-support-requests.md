After a service order or support request is created, it's managed and tracked in the portal where it was initiated. The available tracking experience depends on the request type.

- **Service orders** can be viewed and tracked in both the Surface Management Portal and the Surface Support Portal, regardless of where they were created.
- **Support requests** can be tracked only in the portal where they were created—either the Surface Management Portal or the Surface Support Portal.

To track a service order or support request:

- Open the appropriate portal where the original request was created.
- View your active service orders or support requests.
- Review status indicators such as **Submitted**, **In progress**, **Shipped**, or **Completed**.
- Complete any required follow-up actions.

This approach ensures flexibility for service order tracking across portals while maintaining a consistent, portal-specific experience for support requests.
