Microsoft Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) is a Copilot+ PC built by Microsoft that is built for modern business. As a laptop-tablet hybrid device, it offers the portability of a tablet and the power of a laptop. Combining software, hardware, and AI features, Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) allows you to stay productive wherever work takes place. Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) enables business users to execute their daily tasks with hardware, software, and AI enhancements.

## Hardware

Imagine that you're a business owner who needs a device that enables you to get work done from any location. Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) offers hardware that helps you accomplish your tasks from anywhere. Let's review some of these hardware capabilities that address these needs without adding complexity.

- **Powerful processors:** Modern work requires silicon that can handle the demands of dynamic workspaces. Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) features powerful processors that are optimized for AI workflows. Powered by Intel® Core™ Ultra (Series 3) processors, Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) offers AI performance across creative, analytical, and collaborative work. These processors are engineered for mobility without sacrificing performance. They feature the latest Performance-core, Efficient-core, and Low Power Efficient-core architecture from Intel and a built-in Neural Processing Unit (NPU) with 50 trillions of operations per second (TOPS) that enables Copilot+ PC AI features. By combining a mixture of cores, business users have performance that can last throughout the workday—packing the latest core architecture into a single processor that delivers both power and efficiency.
- **Color accurate display:** For mobile workers, device flexibility regardless of workspace is crucial, and having a versatile display is key to achieving this. The display on Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) is powered by PixelSense™ technology, featuring a 2880x1920 resolution and a dynamic refresh rate up to 120Hz for smooth scrolling experiences. Visuals are clear in multiple scenarios with precise color reproduction, adaptive color and contrast, anti-reflective technology, and high-dynamic range (HDR). It also features a 3:2 aspect ratio, ideal for viewing and interacting with vertically oriented content—displaying more text in Microsoft Office applications like Microsoft Outlook and Word.

>[!NOTE]
> Select Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) models offer an OLED display option with a 1M:1 contrast ratio. Both OLED and LCD versions have been designed to minimize unwanted reflections and has been certified by TÜV SÜD to meet the requirements of ISO 9241-307.

- **Ports and charging:** As a business user looking to get work done from anywhere, port selection is crucial. Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) features two USB-C ports with USB4® functionality—offering support for up to three external monitors for a full desktop setup. Charging can be done via USB-C or the Surface Connect port—supporting 65W charging through Surface Connect and 60W charging through USB-C.

>[!NOTE]
> External monitor support requires compatible displays, cables, and accessories. See the [Surface tested peripherals page](/surface/surface-tested-peripherals) for compatibility information.

- **Connectivity:** To keep up with the needs of modern business, your device needs to be equipped with updated connectivity technology. Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) features Wi-Fi 7, offering stable internet speeds, lower latency, and higher network capacity—ensuring that business users can access their most important documents wherever their work takes them. Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) also features Bluetooth® 5.4 and Bluetooth® Low Energy (LE) enabling fast and reliable connections to essential accessories like keyboards and mice. Select Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) models also feature optional 5G connectivity—allowing you to access cloud resources and real-time communication when Wi-Fi is unavailable.

>[!NOTE]
> 5G connectivity option available with select configurations. Compatibility and performance depend on carrier, network, plan, and other factors.

- **Cameras:** In a world with meetings on Microsoft Teams, it's crucial to have a camera that allows business users to appear their best. Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) features a 1440p Quad HD ultrawide front Surface Studio camera with Windows Studio Effects that delivers clear, well-framed video calls, while the 10MP Ultra HD rear camera captures documents and surroundings in sharp detail.
- **Input methods:** Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) allows users to interact naturally with Windows and Copilot via multiple input methods. Users can interact with the 10-point multi-touch screen, trackpad, voice recognition, or with precise pen input using Surface Slim Pen. These various input methods enable business users to have device flexibility to match their workflow. (Accessories are sold separately)

## Software

In our scenario, you're a small business owner looking to get work done from multiple locations. On-device Copilot+ PC AI features on Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) help to accomplish more tasks efficiently. Let's explore a couple of these features.

- **Recall (preview):** Enabling you to quickly find and jump back into what you have seen before on your PC, Recall (preview) is a powerful productivity tool that provides an explorable timeline to find content you previously had opened. With semantic powered search, simply describe how you remember something that was open and Recall (preview) will retrieve the moment you saw it. Recall (preview) can find photos, files, links, or messages as a fresh point to start from.
- **Windows Studio Effects:** A feature that enables you to get a perfect look for video calls without the need for extra lights, expensive lenses, or sound-proof rooms. Windows Studio Effects automatically adjusts lighting and cancels noises during video call—working in concert with the ultrawide camera and Dual Studio Mics on Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) for a robust video conferencing experience. Studio Effects uses the NPU to deliver a range of audio and visual enhancements that elevate your virtual presence.
- **Custom AI workflows:** Foundry on Windows, Copilot Studio, and Copilot Agents enable organizations to design and extend AI-powered workflows that align with their business requirements. These tools support customization by allowing teams to connect AI capabilities with existing applications, data, and processes—ensuring AI solutions fit established workflows rather than requiring users to adapt how they work. This approach makes it easier to integrate AI into everyday tasks while maintaining control and consistency across the organization.

## Security

Security is a top priority for small business owners and IT teams. Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) delivers improved security and control for peace of mind, fortified with trusted Microsoft protection at every layer.

- **Integrated TPM (Trusted Platform Module) 2.0:** Helps to protect sensitive information and enables Windows security features like BitLocker, Windows Hello for Business, and Windows Defender System Guard. Microsoft Pluton provides a platform for future security updates. TPM 2.0 also enables a fast, secure device with app logins with a built-in fingerprint power button, Windows Hello for Business, and Enhanced Sign-In Security (ESS).
- **Secured-core PC:** Windows integrates closely with Surface hardware to deliver protections that take advantage of hardware capabilities. As a Secured-core PC, Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) applies TPM 2.0 for a hardware root of trust, secure boot, and BitLocker drive encryption. This hardware provides protection against malware, hardware threats, and unauthorized access.
- **Device Firmware Configuration Interface (DFCI):** Designed to be used with software-level mobile device management (MDM), DFCI enables IT admins to remotely disable specific hardware components and prevent end users from accessing them. For example, if you need to protect sensitive information in highly secure areas, you can disable the camera, and if you don't want users booting from USB drives, you can disable that as well.
- **Data transfer and repair:** Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) offers removable internal storage that simplifies data retention for device transfer and decommissioning. A QR code is also included, linking to the device's serial number to simplify tracking and management. On-site repairs for serviceable components are made easier thanks to wayfinding icons and replaceable components.
- **Windows Autopilot:** Deploying Surface Pro for Business, 13-inch (12th Edition) is simplified for IT teams with Windows Autopilot. This feature simplifies the Windows device lifecycle, for both IT and end users, from initial deployment to end of life. Autopilot streamlines IT deployment processes, minimizes infrastructure maintenance requirements, and enhances usability for end users.
