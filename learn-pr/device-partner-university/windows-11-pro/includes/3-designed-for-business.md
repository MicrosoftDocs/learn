## Notepad (enhanced)

Create, edit, and store text files, such as creating notes, editing HTML codes, and much more using Notepad. With new functionality, you can easily generate content or expand on ideas by prompting Notepad with a simple suggestion or bullet points. 

Notepad now supports rich formatting in plaintext files, including headings, lists, bold, italics, and blockquotes. You can also use the new summarize feature to quickly turn your notes into a clear summary. 

Plus, you can now sign in with your Microsoft Entra ID and seamlessly access AI features and be compliant with your organization’s security policies.<sup>1,7</sup>

## Copilot Key

Get productive with the Copilot key on your PC keyboard.<sup>10</sup> When users are signed in with Microsoft Entra ID, pressing the key opens Microsoft 365 to access productivity apps and Microsoft Copilot. IT admins can configure key behavior on managed devices, and users can personalize the key in Settings to open signed apps such as Microsoft Search.

## Windows Studio Effects 

Improve videoconferencing and system performance with AI-enhanced options powered by select Windows 11 Pro PCs with a built-in NPU. You can now access Windows Studio Effects directly from Quick settings on the taskbar, making it quick and easy to turn on and configure effects. 

The features available depend on the original equipment manufacturer and the neural processing unit's processing power. Such features might include enhanced background blur, natural eye contact, automatic framing, and voice focus.

Copilot+ PCs provide access to the complete suite of Windows Studio Effects. *Advanced features that require a 40+ TOPS NPU on Copilot+ PCs include:*<sup>11</sup>

- Portrait light 
- Creative filters  
- Voice enhanced focus
- Portrait blur
- Eye contact teleprompter
- Windows Studio Effects in Quick settings

## Narrator

For enhanced usability, you can use voice commands with the Narrator screen reader. In addition to controlling your device, navigating Windows, and dictating text in nearly any app, you can ask Narrator to read text, edit content, change settings, search commands, and interact with elements like hyperlinks in documents or webpages. 

You can also use Voice Access with Narrator to perform tasks such as opening apps, dictating and editing text, selecting controls, and navigating windows—all using your voice.
