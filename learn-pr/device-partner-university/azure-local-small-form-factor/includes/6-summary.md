In this module, you learned to evaluate whether small form factor deployments of Azure Local (preview) fit your scenario, describe how they work at a high level, and identify when this approach is and isn't the right fit.

For Contoso Manufacturing, this means determining whether deploying compact, locally managed infrastructure at each factory can improve how production lines are monitored, how defects are detected, and how systems respond to operational events.

By understanding these concepts, you're now ready to evaluate whether these small form factor deployments align with your own environments, based on whether they require:

- Low latency
- Distributed infrastructure
- Consistent management across physical locations

Use small form factor deployments of Azure Local (preview) when your scenario requires real-time processing near physical systems, consistent management across distributed locations, and the ability to continue operating during connectivity interruptions.
