As a sales professional, your day often includes customer meetings, follow-ups, prospecting, and managing active opportunities. Staying organized while balancing outreach and customer conversations is essential for maintaining momentum and closing deals.

Copilot can help you prioritize activities, organize your schedule, and prepare for important customer interactions so you can focus on building relationships and advancing opportunities.

> [!TIP]
> Use Copilot to identify the activities that have the greatest impact on customer relationships and active opportunities, helping you spend less time organizing work and more time selling.

:::image type="content" source="../media/copilot-chat-home.png" alt-text="Screenshot of Microsoft 365 Copilot Chat helping prioritize meetings, follow-ups, and daily sales activities.":::

## Prioritize sales activities

To prioritize your day, you can ask Copilot to review your schedule, communications, and customer information.

Example prompt:

"Review my meetings and emails for today and identify the highest-priority customer follow-ups and opportunities I should focus on."

Copilot can help you:

- Identify high-priority opportunities.
- Uncover important customer follow-ups.
- Focus on activities that can have the greatest impact on customer engagement and sales outcomes.

## Block time for focused selling

Balancing meetings with dedicated selling time is essential for maintaining productivity throughout the day.

You can use Copilot to organize your calendar and create time for outreach, customer preparation, and proposal work.

### Example prompt to block time for focused selling

*"Help me organize my calendar today by grouping meetings and reserving focused time for customer outreach and proposal work"*

Copilot can help you:

- Reserve dedicated time for prospecting and outreach.
- Create space for proposal and account preparation.
- Reduce interruptions during focused work.

> [!NOTE]
> The quality of Copilot's suggestions depends on the information it can access, such as your emails, calendar, and customer notes. The more relevant and up-to-date your data, the more useful the results.

## Prepare for customer conversations

Before engaging with a customer, it's important to understand recent interactions, open opportunities, and any outstanding action items.

Copilot can help you quickly review customer information and identify relevant topics before your conversation.

### Example prompt to prepare for a customer meeting

*"Summarize my recent interactions with Contoso, identify open action items, and highlight key discussion points for my upcoming meeting."*

Copilot can help you:

- Review recent customer interactions.
- Identify open action items and opportunities.
- Prepare relevant discussion topics before the meeting.

> [!TIP]
> Try using these example prompts with your own meetings, emails, and opportunities. You can adapt them to reflect your customers, priorities, and sales activities.

By starting your day with a clear plan, dedicated focus time, and relevant customer insights, you can prioritize high-impact activities, strengthen customer engagement, and keep opportunities moving forward with greater confidence.

Preparing for customer interactions is only the first step. Once you've identified your priorities, the next challenge is gathering the information needed to engage customers effectively. Copilot can help you research accounts, understand customer priorities, and create content that supports more meaningful conversations.
