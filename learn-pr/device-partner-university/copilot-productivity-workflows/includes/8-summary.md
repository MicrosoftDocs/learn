Microsoft 365 Copilot helps business users improve productivity by bringing AI directly into the apps they already use—such as Word, Excel, Outlook, Teams, and PowerPoint. Instead of switching between tools, users can stay within their existing workflows to summarize content, analyze data, draft communications, and create deliverables more efficiently.

Across common business scenarios—like reviewing documents, analyzing spreadsheets, catching up on emails, or summarizing meetings—Copilot provides contextual assistance grounded in organizational data. This enables users to quickly move from information to action with greater clarity and reduced manual effort.

A key advantage of Microsoft 365 Copilot is its seamless integration across apps, allowing users to carry context from one step to the next. For example, meeting insights from Teams can be turned into follow-up emails in Outlook, structured documents in Word, and presentations in PowerPoint—all without recreating work.

By keeping AI embedded within Microsoft 365, Copilot reduces friction, maintains security and compliance, and enables teams to focus on decision-making rather than repetitive tasks—resulting in a more seamless and efficient workflow from start to finish.
