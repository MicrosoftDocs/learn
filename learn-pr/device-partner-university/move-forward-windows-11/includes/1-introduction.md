This module is designed to guide you through the upcoming Windows 10 end of support (EOS) and help you prepare your organization to upgrade to Windows 11 Pro with confidence. You’ll learn about the business value of Windows 11 Pro, security benefits, deployment strategies, and productivity enhancements—all tailored to help your organization stay secure, productive, and competitive.


## Table of contents

In this module, you’ll explore what Windows 10 end of support (EOS) means for your organization, why upgrading now is critical, and the key benefits of moving to Windows 11 Pro. You’ll also learn about the tools and resources available to streamline your upgrade journey. 

For organizations needing more time to complete their transition, we’ll briefly cover how Extended Security Updates (ESUs) can serve as a temporary safeguard during the upgrade process.


## Learning objectives

By the end of this module, you'll be able to:

- Understand the implications of Windows 10 EOS  
- Identify the benefits of Windows 11 Pro for your organization  
- Discover tools that simplify deployment and device management  
- Plan a smooth upgrade path using available Microsoft resources
