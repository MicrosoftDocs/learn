AI is reshaping how work gets done across many industries. From automating routine tasks to surfacing insights that support decision-making, AI tools help teams move faster and focus on higher-value work.

## Why AI-ready devices matter

As AI becomes part of everyday workflows, the devices professionals use need to keep pace. Organizations consistently rank improving productivity as a top priority.

However, productivity challenges persist. Many professionals spend significant portions of their day searching for information, switching between tools, or waiting on slow processes. These are the types of tasks that on-device AI can help address.

Copilot+ PCs bring AI processing directly to the device through a dedicated neural processing unit (NPU). This enables AI-powered features to run locally—supporting faster performance, improved responsiveness, and reduced reliance on cloud connectivity.

Building on these productivity challenges, Copilot+ PCs introduce capabilities designed to support everyday work more efficiently.

## Who benefits from Copilot+ PCs?

Copilot+ PCs provide value across a wide range of roles and industries. Professionals in areas such as sales, marketing, customer service, and creative work can benefit from improved speed, responsiveness, and content creation capabilities.

More broadly, any role that involves managing information, communicating with others, or making time-sensitive decisions can benefit from on-device AI.
