In this module, you learned how 5G on Surface for Business devices helps users stay securely connected, supports mobile productivity, and gives organizations flexible options for configuring and managing cellular connectivity.

## Key takeaways

- 5G on Surface for Business provides fast, reliable connectivity that helps users stay productive in hybrid, mobile, and field-based work environments.
- Surface devices are engineered for strong 5G performance through antenna placement, materials design, and testing across real-world environments and global mobile networks.
- Organizations can use physical nano SIM or eSIM options to configure cellular connectivity based on user needs and deployment scenarios.
- 5G on Surface also supports secure access, centralized management, and industry-specific workflows that benefit from always-on connectivity.
