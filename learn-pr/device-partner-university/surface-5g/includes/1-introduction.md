In today's business environment, being adaptable is crucial to staying efficient. Hybrid workspaces have never been more common, requiring device hardware that moves at the pace of your work. Select Surface for Business devices feature 5G technology as a solution to make this context switching more seamless. In this module, you'll learn about 5G on Surface devices and how it can help to improve productivity and keep business users connected.

## Learning objectives

By the end of this module, you'll be able to:

- Compare the benefits of 5G vs Wi-Fi for mobile business scenarios.
- Configure cellular connectivity using nano SIM vs eSIM.
- Select the appropriate connectivity option based on user needs.
- Describe how 5G supports security and device management with Intune.
