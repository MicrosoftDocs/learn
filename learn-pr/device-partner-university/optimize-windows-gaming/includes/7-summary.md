In this module, you followed the scenario of a new PC gamer who is looking for a way to find all their games in one place, and to access tools that help them get more from each play experience.

- You learned how to launch games from multiple platforms using the Xbox app.
- You improved your gameplay experience with features built into the Xbox app and Windows.
- You connected with friends and captured favorite moments without interrupting your play session.

You're now ready to use the Xbox app as a central hub and command center for better Windows gaming experiences.
