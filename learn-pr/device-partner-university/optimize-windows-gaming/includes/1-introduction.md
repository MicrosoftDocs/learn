PC gamers can play a huge range of games: from exciting action blockbusters, to stirring roleplaying game adventures, to mind-bending puzzle challenges. That variety comes from an equally broad ecosystem, as many gamers use multiple platforms—such as Xbox, Steam, the Epic Games Store, and others—to access their favorite content.

While the wide selection of places to find titles is part of what makes PC gaming so vibrant, it can also make it harder to access. Gaming libraries can become fragmented across multiple platforms, each requiring their own dedicated stores and launcher apps. The tools each platform offers to manage system-level features, enable social sharing, and improve performance might be similarly scattered—making it harder to get the most out of each gaming experience.

In this module, you'll follow the scenario of a PC gamer looking for an easy way to access games and features that enhance them. Following their perspective, you'll learn how to optimize your Windows gaming experience using one place to launch games, manage performance, and connect with other players.

## Learning objectives

By the end of this module, you'll be able to:

- **Use the Xbox app** as a unified launcher for games across multiple platforms
- **Improve your gameplay** experience using Windows and Xbox app features
- **Connect with friends** and capture experiences without leaving your game
