Microsoft Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) is a Copilot+ PC built by Microsoft. As the lightest model in the Surface Laptop range, it offers added portability to get work done from any location. By combining software, hardware, and AI features, Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) simplifies the completion of routine tasks and enables business users to execute goals with the latest generation of hardware and software advancements.

## Hardware

Imagine your IT team needs to equip project managers with a laptop that balances performance and flexibility. Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) offers hardware that fits these needs. Let's look at some of the device specifications that solve this need without adding complexity.

- **Processor**: Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) is available with two different processor options: Intel® Core™ Ultra 5 325 processor and Qualcomm® Snapdragon X Plus (8 Core) X1P-42-100. Both processers offer long battery life while enabling on-device AI experiences and workflows. Both configurations feature an NPU (Neural Processing Unit) capable of up to 50 TOPS (trillions of operations per second)—enabling local AI models to run directly on the device. This hardware combination enables various on-device AI experiences, including document summaries, multilingual translation, and multi-monitor setups to complete work.

>[!NOTE]
> Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) with the Intel® Core™ Ultra 5 features an NPU with 50 TOPS support while Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) with Qualcomm® Snapdragon X Plus features an NPU with 45 TOPS support.

- **13" PixelSense Display**: Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) features a 13" display with 1920 x 1280 resolution with PixelSense™ technology, offering touch input in addition to using peripherals. It features a 3:2 aspect ratio, ideal for vertical content—displaying more data in apps like Microsoft Word and Outlook. The screen features 10-point multi-touch and antireflective technology with adaptive color and contrast, allowing for use in various lighting conditions.
- **Port and charging:** Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) features two USB-C ports that support charging, data transfer, DisplayPort (supporting up to two 4K monitors at 60Hz), and Surface Thunderbolt 4 dock and other accessories. Charging on this model is done via one of the USB-C ports—supporting fast charging at 60W. In addition to the USB-C ports, Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) also features one USB-A port and a 3.5-mm headphone port for wired audio.

## Software

In our scenario, your IT team is looking for a solution that places a focus on performance. On-device Copilot+ PC AI features on Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) help users accomplish their tasks.

- **Improved Windows Search:** On Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition), searching for essential files is now simplified with improved Windows Search. Built into File Explorer, the Windows Search box on the taskbar, and in Settings, Windows Search helps you find what you are looking for by utilizing natural language terms instead of having to remember the full file name and location. This feature also works for system settings—including adding new devices using everyday language (for example "add headphones").
- **Live Captions:** An accessibility feature that translates audio from 40 different languages into English captions and 27 languages in Simplified Chinese. Live Captions can be turned on in Accessibility under Captions and works with a wide range of media—including streaming webinars, video calls, and local files.
- **Custom AI workflows:** Foundry on Windows, Copilot Studio, and Copilot Agents enable organizations to design and extend AI-powered workflows that align with their business requirements. These tools support customization by allowing teams to connect AI capabilities with existing applications, data, and processes—ensuring AI solutions fit established workflows rather than requiring users to adapt how they work. This approach makes it easier to integrate AI into everyday tasks while maintaining control and consistency across the organization.

## Security

Security is a top priority for IT teams and small business owners. Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) delivers security and control for peace of mind, fortified with trusted Microsoft protection at every layer.

- **Integrated TPM (Trusted Platform Module) 2.0:** Helps to protect sensitive information and enables Windows security features like BitLocker, Windows Hello for Business, and Windows Defender System Guard. Microsoft Pluton provides a platform for future security updates. TPM 2.0 also enables a fast, secure device with app logins with a built-in fingerprint power button, Windows Hello for Business, and Enhanced Sign-In Security (ESS).
- **Secured-core PC:** Windows integrates closely with Surface hardware to deliver protections that take advantage of hardware capabilities. As a Secured-core PC, Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) uses TPM 2.0 for a hardware root of trust, secure boot, and BitLocker device encryption. This hardware provides protection against malware, hardware threats, and unauthorized access.
- **Device Firmware Configuration Interface (DFCI):** Designed to be used with software-level mobile device management (MDM), DFCI enables IT admins to remotely disable specific hardware components and prevent end users from accessing them. For example, if you need to protect sensitive information in highly secure areas, you can disable the camera, and if you don't want users booting from USB drives, you can disable that as well.
- **Data transfer and repair:** Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) offers removeable internal storage that simplifies data retention for device transfer and decommissioning. A QR code is also included, linking to the device's serial number to simplify tracking and management. On-site repairs for serviceable components are made easier thanks to wayfinding icons and replaceable components.
- **Windows Autopilot:** Deploying Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) is simplified for IT teams with Windows Autopilot. This feature simplifies the Windows device lifecycle, for both IT and end users, from initial deployment to end of life. Autopilot streamlines IT deployment processes, minimizes infrastructure maintenance requirements, and enhances usability for end users.
