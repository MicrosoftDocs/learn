Selecting the right laptop for your business can be challenging, and it's important to consider how and where people work. This module introduces Microsoft Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition), a Copilot+ PC built by Microsoft and designed for portability first and standardized deployment scenarios across the Surface lineup. It supports a range of everyday usage needs, including information work and higher education applications.

### Learning objectives

- Evaluate whether Microsoft Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) is appropriate for your business.
- Describe how the software and hardware features of Microsoft Surface Laptop for Business, 13-inch (1st Edition) work to enable AI workflows.
