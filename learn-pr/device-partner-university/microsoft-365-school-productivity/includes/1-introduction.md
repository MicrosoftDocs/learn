Learn how Microsoft 365 and Copilot can help you organize assignments, research topics, and draft academic work so you can stay focused, manage your time more effectively, and stay on track throughout the day.

## Scenario

Meet Alex. A new school term is beginning, and Alex is preparing to manage classes, assignments, group projects, and upcoming exams.

Throughout the day, Alex needs to:

- Keep track of deadlines across multiple subjects.
- Take and organize notes during classes.
- Research and complete assignments.
- Collaborate with classmates on shared work.
- Prepare for upcoming exams.

Managing all of these responsibilities can feel overwhelming without a clear system for staying organized.

## The challenge

Throughout the day, students move between planning, researching, writing, collaborating, and studying. Keeping track of assignments, deadlines, and class materials can become difficult as responsibilities increase.

Without an effective workflow, it's easy to lose track of priorities, miss important information, or spend extra time completing academic tasks. Over time, this situation can lead to increased stress, missed deadlines, and reduced quality of work.

## What you learn

In this module, you follow a day-in-the-life student scenario and explore how the combination of Microsoft 365 and Copilot can help you stay organized, complete academic tasks, and manage your workload more effectively.

## Learning objectives

By the end of this module, you're able to:

- Organize academic responsibilities by using Microsoft 365.
- Plan and prioritize assignments in Word.
- Research and organize information by using Microsoft 365 experiences.
- Use Copilot and Editor to support drafting and reviewing academic work.
- Apply Microsoft 365 experiences throughout a typical school day.
