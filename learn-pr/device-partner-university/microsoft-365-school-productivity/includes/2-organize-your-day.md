It's Monday morning, and Alex has a full schedule of classes, assignments, and activities.

Before starting the first assignment, Alex takes a few minutes to review upcoming responsibilities and create a plan for the day.

## Start your day with a plan

Creating a plan can help you:

- Keep track of assignments and deadlines.
- Focus on the most important work first.
- Reduce the stress of managing multiple responsibilities.
- Stay organized as new tasks and priorities arise.

A clear plan makes it easier to stay productive throughout the school day.

## Review priorities and deadlines

As Alex reviews the week ahead, several responsibilities need attention:

- Complete a history essay draft.
- Finish math homework.
- Review notes from today's science class.
- Gather research for an upcoming assignment.
- Prepare for a biology quiz.

Looking at everything at once can feel overwhelming.

Instead, break larger responsibilities into smaller, manageable tasks.

For example:

| Assignment or responsibility | Smaller task |
| ---------------------------- | ------------ |
| History essay | Create an outline |
| Biology quiz | Review key concepts |
| Group project | Prepare discussion topics |
| Science notes | Summarize key takeaways |

Breaking work into smaller steps makes it easier to understand where to begin and track progress throughout the day.

## Use Copilot to help prioritize work

When reviewing assignments, it isn't always obvious where to begin. Copilot can help summarize information, identify next steps, and organize ideas.

For example, Alex might enter the following prompt in Copilot:

*Summarize my science notes and identify the topics I should review before Friday's quiz.*

Copilot can help surface important information and make it easier to decide what work should be prioritized.

## Stay flexible throughout the day

Plans can change as new assignments, activities, and responsibilities arise.

Throughout the day, Alex reviews priorities and adjusts plans when needed.

Planning isn't about creating a perfect schedule. It's about having a system that helps you stay organized and focused as responsibilities change.
