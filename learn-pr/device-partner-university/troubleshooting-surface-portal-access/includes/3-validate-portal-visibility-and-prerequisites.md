Next, let's explore how to address access issues. Validation is meant to rule out common misconfiguration, not to fix the issues—helping in readiness for potential escalations. When a portal isn't visible or functionality is missing, validate the following before escalating the issue:

- Confirm that you're signed into the correct tenant.
- Confirm that the required administrative roles are assigned.
- **For Surface Management Portal only:** Confirm that the Surface device is enrolled in Intune.
- Confirm the appropriate Admin Center is being used. On Surface Management Portal this is the Microsoft Intune Admin Center, and on Surface Support Portal it's the Microsoft 365 Admin Center.

>[!NOTE]
> No remediation beyond validation is performed in this module. If validation confirms a missing prerequisite that you can't resolve, escalation is required.
