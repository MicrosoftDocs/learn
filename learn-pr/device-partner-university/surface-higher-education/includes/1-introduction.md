At the higher education level, it's crucial to have the right tools and devices to create a successful digital campus—whether it is for students, faculty, or administrators. Devices play a critical role in how teaching, learning, and administration happen across a campus, influencing accessibility, collaboration, and student outcomes. Microsoft Surface devices help higher education users reach their goals and ensure that evolving educational needs are met.

In this module, you'll discover how Surface devices accelerate learning, simplify IT, and improve efficiency. You'll also explore scenarios for all levels of the higher education equation—students, faculty, administrators—to better understand why Surface devices help higher education users be successful.

## Learning objectives

By the end of this module, you'll be able to:

- Explain how Surface devices support hybrid instruction and inclusive learning across higher education roles.
- Identify the core benefits of Surface in higher education and match them to common academic workflows.
- Evaluate the end-to-end experience of Surface with Windows and Microsoft 365 and how it simplifies teaching, learning, and administrative tasks.
- Differentiate the needs of students, educators, and administrators and select Surface features that address each user's top priorities.
