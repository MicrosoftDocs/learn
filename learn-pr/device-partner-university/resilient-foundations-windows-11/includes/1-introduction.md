For modern business users, it's essential to utilize tools that provide real-world benefits that lead to results. With Windows 11 Pro PCs, businesses can increase operational efficiency, onboard remote employees efficiently, and enforce company security policies across PCs and AI tools.

In this module, you'll discover how Windows 11 Pro provides performance, reliability, and resiliency to business users. You'll also explore key scenarios to better understand how Windows 11 Pro devices and features can maximize business productivity and efficiency.

## Learning objectives:

By the end of this module, you'll be able to:

- Explain the basics of Windows 11 Pro for performance and reliability.
- Describe resiliency features and their role in business continuity.
- Configure and manage recovery and update automation using Microsoft Intune and Autopatch.
- Apply best practices for securing mission-critical operations.
