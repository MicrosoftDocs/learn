Live Captions is most valuable in situations where audio alone isn’t enough.

Consider using Live Captions when:

- **Audio is difficult to hear** due to noise or poor quality
- **You’re working in a quiet environment** where sound can't be played
- **Content doesn’t include built-in subtitles**
- **You want to better follow conversations or presentations**
- **You need support understanding different accents or languages**

In these scenarios, Live Captions provide an immediate way to stay informed without interrupting your workflow.

**Quick takeaway**: Use Live Captions whenever audio is difficult to hear, unavailable, or hard to follow.
