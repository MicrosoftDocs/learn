Understanding spoken content quickly and accurately is essential for maintaining productivity. Even small barriers—such as unclear audio or missing subtitles—can interrupt workflow and reduce efficiency.

Live Captions help remove these barriers by making audio content immediately accessible as text. This helps users:

- **Stay focused without replaying content**
- **Follow conversations more easily**
- **Reduce misunderstandings in meetings**
- **Work effectively in noisy or quiet environments**

Live Captions also supports accessibility by helping individuals who are deaf or hard of hearing better engage with digital content and conversations.

On Copilot+ PCs, Live Captions can also enable real-time translation, allowing users to follow content spoken in other languages. This expands access to information and supports more inclusive communication across global teams.
