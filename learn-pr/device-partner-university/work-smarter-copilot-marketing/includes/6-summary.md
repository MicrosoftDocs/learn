In this module, you explored how marketing professionals can use Copilot in Microsoft 365 apps together with Copilot+ PC experiences to move through common work moments more efficiently. You learned how to consolidate campaign research into a creative brief, turn strategy into a first-draft presentation, refine content for the right audience, locate supporting assets, stay engaged in stakeholder meetings, capture follow-up actions, and prepare for the next workday.

Ultimately, this combined ecosystem ensures you can seamlessly transition from planning and creating to reviewing and aligning without traditional workflow friction.

## Key takeaways

- Copilot in Word can help marketing teams synthesize research, customer insights, and campaign inputs into a concise creative brief.
- Copilot in PowerPoint can transform a completed brief into a structured first-draft presentation that teams can review and refine.
- Copilot+ PC experiences such as Click to Do, Improved Windows Search, Live Captions, Windows Studio Effects, and Agent in Settings can reduce friction across daily marketing workflows.
- Copilot in Teams and Outlook can help capture decisions, identify action items, draft follow-up communications, and summarize end-of-day progress.
- Using clear, specific prompts helps Copilot produce more useful outputs that support planning, stakeholder alignment, and follow-through.
- Choosing the right tool depends on your workspace: rely on cloud-powered Copilot within Microsoft 365 apps to generate, analyze, and build marketing assets, and use on-device Copilot+ PC experiences to quickly find files, adjust system settings, and streamline your local desktop environment.
