Copilot+ PCs bring business-ready AI to every department—unlocking new efficiencies and delivering better outcomes with AI-powered features and solutions designed for real-world results.

In this module, you'll explore how the combination of on-device Copilot+ PC AI experiences and Microsoft 365 (sold separately) helps turn everyday marketing tasks into competitive advantages. While you'll use cloud-powered Copilot in Microsoft 365 apps to draft campaign copies, analyze spreadsheet data, or design presentation decks, you'll apply on-device Copilot+ PC experiences to quickly recall lost creative assets, generate localized visual ideas, and streamline local workflows without missing a beat.

## Learning objectives

By the end of this module you'll be able to:

- Use Copilot in Word to transform marketing research and customer insights into structured creative briefs.
- Use Copilot in PowerPoint to generate presentation drafts from marketing briefs and campaign plans.
- Apply Copilot+ PC experiences such as Click to Do and Improved Windows Search to refine content and locate supporting assets more efficiently.
- Use Copilot and Copilot+ PC features to improve communication, collaboration, and stakeholder engagement during meetings and reviews.
