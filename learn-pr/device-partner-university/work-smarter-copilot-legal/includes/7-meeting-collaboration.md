## Stay engaged with Live Captions

:::image type="content" source="../media/captions.png" alt-text="Screenshot of Live Captions displaying spoken content from a streaming video." lightbox="../media/captions-expanded.png":::

During meetings, it can be difficult to keep track of every detail when multiple people are speaking, technical terms are used, or conversations move quickly. Live Captions can help you follow spoken conversations with on-screen text, making it easier to review important details, identify actions, and stay aligned with participants.

Live Captions can provide real-time captions for audio on your device. On Copilot+ PCs, Live Captions can also support translation for certain languages, which can be helpful when working with international partners.

Do the following to use Live Captions:

1. Start or join your meeting.
2. Press Windows key + Ctrl + L to turn on Live Captions.
3. Follow the conversation with real-time captions on your screen.

Live Captions can help you follow conversations more clearly during legal and business discussions. Real-time captions make it easier to capture important details, while translation capabilities support collaboration with global teams.

**When to use**: Use Live Captions to follow conversations as they happen. After the meeting, use Copilot in Teams to summarize key points, capture decisions, and organize follow-up actions.

> [!NOTE]
> Availability and capabilities vary by device, market, region, language, and configuration. Live Captions translation support and language coverage vary by device and language. On supported Copilot+ PCs, Live Captions translates audio and video subtitles into English from 40+ languages and from 27 languages into Simplified Chinese.

## Look and sound clear with Windows Studio Effects

:::image type="content" source="../media/effects.png" alt-text="Screenshot of Windows Studio Effects with portrait blur selected for a camera feed." lightbox="../media/effects-expanded.png":::

Virtual meetings often require clear communication, especially when discussing contracts, compliance requirements, legal issues, gaps, or areas for legal review, and approvals. Windows Studio Effects can help improve audio and video quality so you can communicate more clearly during these conversations.

Windows Studio Effects includes several options that can help improve your meeting experience. Depending on the effects you use, you can:

- Reduce background distractions.
- Improve audio quality so others can hear you clearly.
- Improve how you appear on camera during meetings.

For legal professionals, the most relevant Windows Studio Effects for virtual meetings often include:

- Background Blur to help reduce visual distractions and keep attention on the speaker.
- Voice Focus to help minimize background noise and improve audio clarity during discussions about contracts, compliance requirements, or legal issues.
- Eye Contact to help maintain engagement when presenting recommendations or discussing complex legal topics with stakeholders.

These are just a few of the available effects. The following table provides a complete list and descriptions of each option.

| **Effect** | **Description** |
| ------------ | ----------------- |
| **Automatic Framing** | Keeps you centered in the camera view as you move during a meeting or call. |
| **Background Blur** | Softens or blurs your background to reduce distractions and keep the focus on you. **Standard Blur** provides a natural background blur. **Portrait Blur** creates a stronger depth-of-field effect similar to portrait mode on a camera. |
| **Eye Contact** | Adjusts your gaze to help you appear more engaged and maintain eye contact during video calls. **Standard** provides subtle gaze correction. **Teleprompter** uses AI to create a more natural eye-contact experience, even when reading content on your screen. |
| **Portrait Light** | Enhances lighting on your face to improve visibility in different lighting conditions. |
| **Creative Filters** | Applies artistic visual effects to your video for a customized appearance. Available filters include **Illustrated**, **Animated**, and **Watercolor**. |
| **Voice Focus** | Reduces background noise and enhances your voice so others can hear you more clearly. |

To use Windows Studio Effects:

1. Press Windows key + A to open Quick Settings.
2. Select the Studio Effects tile.
3. Review available camera and audio options.
4. Turn on the effects you want to use.

Windows Studio Effects can help you communicate more clearly during virtual meetings, contract discussions, and other common legal conversations. By improving video quality and reducing audio distractions, these features can help participants stay focused on the discussion and key decisions.

> [!NOTE]
> Availability and capabilities vary by device, market, region, language, and configuration. Windows Studio Effects requires compatible hardware, and available effects vary by device.

## Capture meeting outcomes with Copilot in Teams

Legal meetings tend to move quickly with questions, decisions, issues, and follow-up actions discussed throughout the conversation. It can be difficult to capture all these details accurately while staying engaged. Copilot in Teams can help you summarize discussions, capture questions, and identify next steps—so you can focus on the conversation instead of taking notes.

Copilot can help you:

- Summarize key questions.
- Identify decisions and agreed positions.
- Draft a meeting recap or status update.
- Organize next steps for review.

Do the following to use Copilot in Teams:

1. Open the meeting chat in Microsoft Teams.
2. Select Copilot.
3. Enter the following prompt before the meeting begins:
  *“Track questions, decisions, and agreed positions. After the meeting, draft a follow-up email outlining guidance and next steps.”*

Copilot can help capture questions, decisions, potential issues, and action items from meetings. This makes it easier to communicate guidance, track follow-up work, and maintain alignment across legal and business stakeholders after the discussion.
