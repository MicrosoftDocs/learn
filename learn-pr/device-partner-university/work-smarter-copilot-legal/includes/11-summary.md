In this module, you explored how Microsoft 365 Copilot and Copilot+ PC experiences can support common legal workflows. You learned how to use these tools to find information, review content, collaborate with stakeholders, communicate more effectively, and organize work throughout the day.

By combining Microsoft 365 Copilot with Copilot+ PC experiences, legal professionals can spend less time on repetitive tasks and more time on high-value work.

>
> **Key takeaways**
>

>- I understand how Microsoft 365 Copilot and Copilot+ PC experiences can be applied to common legal workflows to help legal professionals work more efficiently and focus on higher-value work.
