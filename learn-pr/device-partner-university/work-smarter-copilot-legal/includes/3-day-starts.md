At the beginning of the workday, your goal is to quickly understand what's new, determine priorities, and focus on the work that needs immediate attention.

Copilot in Outlook can help summarize unread messages and organize them by topic, urgency, and areas that might require legal review. This can help you identify which requests need follow-up, which reviews are due soon, and which items might need escalation.

Copilot can help you:

- Identify urgent legal requests.
- Group messages by workstream.
- Highlight upcoming deadlines and possible issues, gaps, or areas that might require legal review.
- Quickly understand what requires immediate attention and what can wait.

To use Copilot in Outlook to catch up on legal priorities:

1. Open Microsoft Outlook.
2. Select the Copilot icon.
3. In the prompt box, enter the following prompt:
  *“Summarize my unread emails from the last 24 hours. Group by contract reviews, compliance questions, and escalations. Flag urgent deadlines and matters that might require legal review.”*

This prompt helps legal professionals move from triaging incoming requests to focused review by bringing the most urgent reviews, deadlines, and escalations into focus.

**When to use**: Use Copilot in Outlook to understand what requires attention first. Use Microsoft 365 Copilot Chat to help organize your schedule, schedule dedicated time for focused work, and prepare for upcoming tasks.

> [!TIP]
> For the best results, specify the type of legal task in your prompt.
>
> For example:
>
>- Approvals
>- Compliance questions
>- Contract reviews
>- Escalations
>- External counsel updates

## Protect focus time for legal analysis

Once you understand your priorities, the next step is to create space for legal review. Tasks like contract analysis, policy review, and compliance assessment require attention to detail, so it's useful to block time and reduce interruptions before you begin.

Microsoft 365 Copilot Chat can help you identify review deadlines and find available time in your calendar for uninterrupted work. On a Copilot+ PC, Agent in Settings can help reduce distractions by allowing you to use natural language to adjust device settings—such as turning on Focus mode or Do Not Disturb.

Use Microsoft 365 Copilot Chat to schedule review time:

1. Go to [Microsoft 365 Copilot Chat](https://m365.cloud.microsoft/chat) and sign in with your Microsoft 365 work account.
2. In the message box, enter the following prompt:
  *“Based on my calendar and review deadlines, block time for contract analysis and reserve time to prepare before stakeholder meetings.”*
3. Copilot helps identify a time block and prepares the calendar details for your review.

> [!NOTE]
> Microsoft 365 Copilot experiences require an eligible Microsoft 365 subscription, license, and organizational permissions. Calendar actions might require confirmation before they’re finalized. Access to calendar data depends on your organization’s tenant configuration and permissions.

Windows includes tools such as Focus mode and Do Not Disturb to help reduce distractions. To use these settings, you typically locate them within the Settings app and configure them manually. On a Copilot+ PC, Agent in Settings lets you describe what you want in natural language, such as *"Turn on Focus mode for 90 minutes,"* and then recommends the appropriate setting change for you to review and apply.

:::image type="content" source="../media/agent-settings.png" alt-text="Screenshot of Agent in Settings increasing display brightness from a natural language request." lightbox="../media/agent-settings-expanded.png":::

Next, use Agent in Settings to reduce notifications and other distractions on your PC:

1. Press Windows key + I to open Settings.
2. In the search box, type:
  *“Turn on Focus mode for 90 minutes.”*
3. Review the suggested setting change.
4. Select Apply to accept the recommended change.

This approach helps you prioritize legal work, reduce distractions, and dedicate uninterrupted time to detailed analysis.

**When to use**: Use Microsoft 365 Copilot Chat when you need to plan time for detailed legal work, such as contract analysis, policy review, or compliance assessment. Use Agent in Settings on your Copilot+ PC to reduce distractions and help maintain a focused work environment during that time.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Availability and capabilities vary by device, market, region, language, and configuration. Agent in Settings might require specific hardware or updates that continue to roll out over time.
