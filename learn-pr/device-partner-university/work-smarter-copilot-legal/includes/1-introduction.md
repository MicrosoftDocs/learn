Copilot+ PCs can help legal departments work more efficiently by supporting common tasks such as contract review, compliance analysis, risk assessment, and stakeholder communications.

In this module, you'll explore how the combination of on-device Copilot+ PC experiences and Microsoft 365 Copilot can help legal professionals move through common workflows more efficiently. Whether you're reviewing contracts, analyzing compliance information, preparing stakeholder guidance, or communicating possible issues and next steps—Copilot can help you summarize information, draft content, and organize follow-up activities. You'll use Microsoft 365 Copilot to review and analyze work content, and Copilot+ PC experiences to quickly find information, refine content, support meetings, and streamline local workflows.
