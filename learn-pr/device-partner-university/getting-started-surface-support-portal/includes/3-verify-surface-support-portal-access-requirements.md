Before using Surface Support Portal, confirm the following:

- A Microsoft tenant exists for your organization.
- You have an appropriate administrative role.
- Intune enrollment isn't required.

For Surface Support Portal users, it isn't required to have a device enrolled in Intune, meaning that this portal can service and support workflows even on unenrolled devices.

Imagine that you're an IT administrator and you need to submit a repair request for a deployed Surface device. A deployed Surface device requires repair, but isn't enrolled in Intune.

**Correct action:** Use Surface Support Portal to initiate service, because Intune enrollment isn't required.
