As organizations adopt AI-powered devices and workflows, users may need a way to explore new experiences and understand how those experiences fit into their everyday work.

The Copilot+ PC Hub provides a guided, interactive experience designed to help users explore Copilot+ PC capabilities at their own pace. Through visual demonstrations, practical examples, and guided workflows, users can discover experiences that support their everyday tasks and responsibilities.

## Copilot+ PC experiences available through the hub

The Copilot+ PC Hub introduces several Copilot+ PC experiences designed to support productivity, communication, and information discovery.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Copilot+ PC experiences vary by device, market, and language and may require updates that continue to roll out over time. Availability and timing may vary. See [Copilot + PCs claims](/windows/deployment/performance-lab/claims-disclosures?tabs=copilot-plus).

Examples include:

- Improved Windows Search for natural language file and content discovery when users remember context but not exact file names
- Click to Do for quick actions and workflow shortcuts while working across content and tasks
- Live Captions with translation for multilingual communication support
- Windows Studio Effects for enhanced video and meeting experiences
- Recall (preview) for rediscovering previously viewed content
- Agent in Settings for natural language device assistance

Because users may adopt Copilot+ PC experiences at different speeds and for different needs, this web experience is designed to support flexible exploration. Some users may begin with communication and meeting experiences, while others may focus on productivity, search, or workflow organization.
