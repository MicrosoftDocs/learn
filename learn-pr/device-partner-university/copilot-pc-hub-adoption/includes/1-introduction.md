Copilot+ PCs are Windows devices designed to support on-device AI experiences that introduce new productivity capabilities and workflows. As users begin exploring these experiences, they may need guidance on where to start, which features are most relevant to their role, and how to continue learning over time.

Whether users are new to Copilot+ PCs or looking to better understand which experiences are most relevant to their role and daily tasks, the Copilot+ PC Hub provides a guided starting point for continued exploration and learning.

The Copilot+ PC Hub provides a central place to explore Copilot+ PC experiences through guided content, visual demonstrations, and role-based scenarios. The Copilot+ PC Hub supports self-paced learning by helping users explore how Copilot+ PC experiences can support everyday work across different business functions.

Throughout the Copilot+ PC Hub, users can explore experiences such as:

- Improved Windows Search
- Click to Do
- Live Captions with translation
- Windows Studio Effects
- Recall (preview)
- Agent in Settings

> [!NOTE]
> This module is designed for users who want to learn how to explore and adopt Copilot+ PC experiences using the Copilot+ PC Hub. It focuses on feature discovery, role-based productivity scenarios, and continued learning rather than device deployment or technical configuration.

In this module, you learn how to use the Copilot+ PC Hub to explore Copilot+ PC experiences and discover workflows relevant to your role and daily tasks.

By the end of this module, you are able to:

- Identify Copilot+ PC experiences available through the hub
- Describe the purpose of the Copilot+ PC Hub
- Explore role-based scenarios and productivity workflows
- Explain how the hub can be used as a continued learning resource for Copilot+ PC adoption
