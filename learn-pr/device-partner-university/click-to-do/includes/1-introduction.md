A lot of everyday work is made up of small actions—copying text from an image, rewriting a sentence, looking up information, or moving content between apps. Individually, these tasks only take a few seconds. But together, they can interrupt focus and slow down momentum.

Click to Do helps reduce those interruptions by letting you take action directly on what’s already on screen. Available on Copilot+ PCs, it uses on-device AI to recognize text and images and suggest relevant actions in context. This means that instead of switching between tools, you can complete tasks where you’re already working and keep moving.

In this module, you’ll learn how to use Click to Do to interact with text and images across your screen. You’ll explore how it works, when to use it, and how it supports real-world workflows.
