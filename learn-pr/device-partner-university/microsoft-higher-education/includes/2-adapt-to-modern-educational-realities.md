In today's higher education landscape, AI is beginning to change how teaching, learning, and academic work are approached, particularly in areas like productivity, accessibility, and student support. Therefore, having the right devices and the right digital strategy is critical to the effectiveness of the higher education experience. By implementing a modern device strategy, higher education institutions can focus more on educational outcomes rather than worrying about the tools used.

Flexible and responsive devices introduce versatility to all higher education users. That versatility can be achieved through form factors, applications, or software. This ensures that users can let their device adapt to them rather than the opposite. Today's higher education users can solve three core issues with a modern device:

- **Accelerate learning and prepare students for future academic and professional pathways:** Modern devices help reduce friction in common academic tasks by streamlining tasks, alleviating workloads, and enhancing capabilities. They can keep administrators and faculty productive while helping students to accelerate their learning with AI-enhanced experiences that support more personalized learning experiences.
- **Simplify and secure IT:** Education institutions are vulnerable to security threats like malware attacks, especially with unmanaged devices on a digital campus. A modern device strategy helps institutions reduce security risk through unified management and data protection policies that align with campus security policies.
- **Improve efficiency:** For operations, placing the right technology in the hands of staff provides cost savings and a path to advance digital innovation and empowers institutions to capitalize on modern technology. Modern Microsoft education solutions turn insights into action and empower higher education institutions to innovate with confidence with a flexible, connected AI-ready foundation.
