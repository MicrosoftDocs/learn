In higher education, it's crucial to have the right tools and devices to create a successful digital campus—whether it is for students, faculty, or administrators. Devices play a critical role in how teaching, learning, and administration happen across a campus, influencing accessibility, collaboration, and student outcomes. The combination of Copilot+ PCs, Windows 11 Pro, and Microsoft 365 help higher education users support reach their goals and support evolving educational needs.

In this module, you'll discover how Microsoft's higher education solutions help accelerate learning, simplify IT, and improve efficiency. You'll also explore scenarios for all levels of the higher education equation—students, faculty, administrators—to better understand why Windows 11 Pro devices help higher education users be successful.

>[!NOTE]
> Availability of Copilot features mentioned in this module depends on institutional licensing and device configuration.

## Learning objectives

By the end of this module, you'll be able to:

- Explain how Copilot+ PCs support hybrid instruction and inclusive learning across higher education roles.
- Identify the core benefits of Microsoft in higher education and match them to common academic workflows.
- Evaluate the end-to-end experience of Copilot+ PCs with Windows 11 Pro and Microsoft 365 and how it simplifies teaching, learning, and administrative tasks.
- Differentiate the needs of students, educators, and administrators and select Windows 11 Pro features that address each user's top priorities.
