In this module, you learned about common cyberthreats like ransomware, how to support business resiliency, and how to design solutions for secure backup, disaster recovery, and security updates. The content in this module helps you prepare for the certification exam SC-100: Microsoft Cybersecurity Architect.

The main takeaways from this module include understanding the threat landscape, attack vectors, and attack chain frameworks. You learned how resilience goals focus on limiting the impact and likelihood of disruptions before, during, and after active attacks to business operations. You also learned how to design ransomware-resilient backup architectures and evaluate security update solutions across hybrid and multicloud environments.

## Learning objectives

In this module, you learned how to:

* Understand common cyberthreats like ransomware.
* Understand how to support business resiliency.
* Design configurations for secure backup and restore.
* Design solutions for managing security updates.
