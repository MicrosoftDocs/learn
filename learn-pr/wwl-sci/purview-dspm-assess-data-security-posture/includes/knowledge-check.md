An organization recently expanded into a new business unit and rolled out Microsoft 365 Copilot to several teams. To assess the resulting risk, it enabled Data Security Posture Management (DSPM) in Microsoft Purview. A data security administrator has two weeks to complete a first-pass posture assessment. The work is to understand where the organization stands, decide which gaps need attention first, and hand a prioritized list to the teams who own remediation. The questions that follow walk through the decisions made along the way.
