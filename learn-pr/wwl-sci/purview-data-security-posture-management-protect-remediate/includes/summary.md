Protection gaps don't stay static. New data sources appear, sharing patterns shift, and AI adoption introduces new exposure. But the cycle is the same each time: identify the gap from an objective's risk indicators, apply the right policy or remediation action, review outcome metrics to confirm it worked, and reassess when conditions change.

In this module, you learned how to:

- Select remediation approaches based on objective risk indicators
- Apply one-click policies for data loss prevention (DLP), sensitivity labels, and Insider Risk Management
- Measure remediation progress through outcome metrics
- Assess and remediate oversharing with data risk assessments

With assessment identifying where risk exists and protection closing those gaps iteratively, Data Security Posture Management (DSPM) provides a continuous operational loop rather than a one-time configuration. The objectives, policies, and metrics you worked with here update as your data estate evolves.
