In this exercise, you clean raw sales data in a Fabric notebook, join it with customer and product tables, apply aggregations and window functions, and write the results to a Delta table. The exercise reinforces the shaping, joining, aggregation, and writing techniques covered in this module.

> [!NOTE]
> You need access to a Fabric-enabled workspace to complete this exercise. For information about a trial license, see [Getting started with Fabric](/fabric/get-started/fabric-trial).

This lab takes approximately **30** minutes to complete.

Launch the exercise and follow the instructions.
