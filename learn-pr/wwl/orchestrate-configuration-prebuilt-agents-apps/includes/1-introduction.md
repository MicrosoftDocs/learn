In today's business environment, organizations seek to leverage artificial intelligence (AI) to automate decisions, accelerate workflows, and enhance user productivity. Microsoft's ecosystem—including Dynamics 365 Finance and Supply Chain, Dynamics 365 Commerce, Dynamics 365 Customer Service, and Microsoft 365 Copilot—offers a comprehensive suite of prebuilt agents and AI features designed to meet these needs. This module provides solution architects with the foundational knowledge and practical frameworks required to orchestrate, configure, and extend these AI-driven experiences across finance, supply chain, customer service, and business productivity scenarios.

You will learn how Copilot and AI capabilities can be deployed as conversational assistants, embedded insights, and automated agents, all governed by robust data, security, and compliance standards. The module explores architectural patterns for orchestrating AI features, integrating Copilot with Power Platform, and ensuring that AI solutions align with operational guardrails and business outcomes.

Key topics include:

- The architecture and orchestration of Copilot features in Dynamics 365 Finance, Supply Chain, and Customer Service applications.
- The deployment and configuration of Microsoft 365 Copilot for Sales and Service, including data readiness, extensibility, and workflow integration.
- The practical design and governance of Microsoft 365 agents for specialized business scenarios.
- Best practices for responsible AI, modular extension, and success measurement.

This module equips you to design and operationalize cohesive, compliant, and scalable AI-enabled business processes—empowering your organization to realize the full value of Microsoft's AI ecosystem.

By the end of this module, you will know:

- The orchestration of AI features and Copilot agents across Dynamics 365 and Microsoft 365 applications.
- Architectural considerations for secure, compliant, and extensible AI solutions.
- Configuration and operationalize prebuilt Copilot agents for finance, supply chain, sales, and service scenarios.
- Best practices for responsible AI, workflow integration, and success measurement.
