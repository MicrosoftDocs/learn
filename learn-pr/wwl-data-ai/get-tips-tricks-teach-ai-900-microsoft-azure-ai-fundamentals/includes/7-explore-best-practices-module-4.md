

AI-900: Module 4 Natural Language Processing (NLP) 

As you are preparing to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module. In this module, students will learn about Natural Language Processing (NLP) and the different ways that NLP works including text analysis, translation, speech to text and text to speech services, and many more. 

This module consists of two lessons:  

- Lesson 1 – Introduction to Natural Language Processing 
- Lesson 2 – Building Natural Language solutions in Azure

Module 4 Tips and Tricks 

- Make sure your microphone is working if you are going to demonstrate the speech to text services 
- Ask students when and where they might have interacted with a bot in the past 
- Think about other real-life use cases of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and use these as examples to explain some of the concepts 
- Use the AI demos to showcase the Text Analytics feature 
- Use the Language Service demo and the custom questions/bot demo in class
