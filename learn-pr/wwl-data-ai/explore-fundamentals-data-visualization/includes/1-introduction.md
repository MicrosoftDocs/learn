Data modeling and visualization is at the heart of business intelligence (BI) workloads supported by large-scale data analytics solutions. Data visualization supports reporting and decision making in organizations.

In this module, you learn about fundamental principles of analytical data modeling and data visualization, using Microsoft Power BI as a platform to explore these principles in action.
