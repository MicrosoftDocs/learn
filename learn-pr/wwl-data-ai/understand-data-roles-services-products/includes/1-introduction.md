As a business professional, you are likely to be familiar with using a spreadsheet to filter, summarize, and analyze data. While a spreadsheet has a multitude of uses, organizations need scaled and robust systems to manage data from many sources, and in different formats. You can make reporting easier by collating the data in a central database.

In this module, you will learn about the systems you can use to store and process your data, and the professional roles required to operate, develop, and maintain these services.

## Learning objectives

After completing this module, you will be able to:

- Describe data systems.
- Understand the fundamentals of Azure Data Services.
- Describe the role and purpose of a data professional.
