You followed the Adventure Works team as they planned and prepared a generative AI support agent, moving from a vague idea to a solution they can build, test, and trust.

Along the way, you learned to:

- Define agent specifications that capture user intent, prompts, capabilities, evaluation, and test data.
- Design and optimize an agent by choosing a model and grounding it in your own data.
- Describe the GenAIOps lifecycle and how it structures an app from exploration to production.
- Identify the tools and frameworks that support each phase of the lifecycle.

Starting with a clear specification, rather than a blank prompt, gives every later decision a target to aim for. When you plan your own generative AI solution, write that specification first.
