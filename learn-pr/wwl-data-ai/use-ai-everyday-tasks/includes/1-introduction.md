AI is no longer just for tech experts and scientists. With tools like Microsoft Copilot, AI is becoming more and more accessible for everyday tasks. Imagine having a personal assistant that can help you draft emails, create reports, and generate creative content like poetry or graphics. AI can make your everyday tasks easier and more efficient, allowing you to focus on what matters to you.

In this module, discover the basics of how generative AI works and how you can use it to be more productive. Whether you're looking to simplify routine tasks or explore new ways to innovate, AI can help you achieve more.
