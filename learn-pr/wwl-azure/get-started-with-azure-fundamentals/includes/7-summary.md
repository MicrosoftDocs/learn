Welcome to the wide world of Azure, where you can invent with purpose. This introduction module outlines the goal of the Azure Fundamentals training and gives you a list of basic topics you'll want to understand before building out your first Azure solution. Additionally, the content is aligned to the study areas for the exam for Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900). As you go through the additional study areas, you'll be learning the basics of Azure, and preparing yourself for the exam at the same time.

![Cloud with a graduation cap on top of it representing the start on your journey to learn about the cloud.](../media/graduation-cap-cloud.png)

Join us as we travel through the capabilities of Azure, explore why you should use cloud computing, and decide why Azure is the best choice for your cloud platform.
