Azure IoT device management covers the full lifecycle of the device from the manufacturer all the way to device retirement.

Suppose you have joined a team that is implementing an Azure IoT solution for the company. The company needs your team to manage the IoT devices that have been deployed in the field. Understanding the various approaches to device management and when each should be applied will help to prepare the team for their upcoming device management responsibilities. You decide to examine the device management patterns and approaches that are available.

In this module, you will:

 -  Review device management concepts.
 -  Examine the device management patterns.
 -  Examine device configuration options using device twins and direct methods.

By the end of this module, you will be able to describe the approaches to device management and when each should be applied.
