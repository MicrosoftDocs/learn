## Scenario

As the migration of the SAP workloads in your company to Azure approaches, you need to finalize a detailed plan. You will utilize the SAP workload planning and deployment checklist to guide your company's deployment team through each phase of the process.

## Learning objectives

You will:

 -  Examine the SAP workload planning and deployment checklist.
 -  Explore the project preparation and planning phase.
 -  Explore the pilot phase.
 -  Explore the non-production phase.
 -  Explore the production preparation phase.
 -  Explore the Go Live phase.
 -  Explore the post production phase.
