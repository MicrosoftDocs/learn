In this lab, you learn how to configure and use Microsoft Defender for Cloud's just-in-time (JIT) virtual machine access to protect your Azure virtual machines (VMs) from unauthorized network access. You enable JIT on a VM, configure custom port rules, and practice requesting access.

> [!NOTE]
> To complete this lab, you need:
> - An [Azure subscription](https://azure.microsoft.com/pricing/purchase-options/azure-account?cid=msft_learn)
> - Microsoft Defender for Servers Plan 2 enabled (covered in the lab environment)

Launch the exercise and follow the instructions.
