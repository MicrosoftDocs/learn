## Summary

Tools-based deployment and management of your solutions is a must whether working on-premises or in the cloud.  In this module, we took a quick look at the tools available to you. Then explored how they can help you build the cloud solutions your business needs.

This module discussed the management topics like:

+ Azure management tools like PowerShell and resource template.
+ Using Azure Advisor to get suggestions about your site.
+ Exploring which tools best meet your management needs.
