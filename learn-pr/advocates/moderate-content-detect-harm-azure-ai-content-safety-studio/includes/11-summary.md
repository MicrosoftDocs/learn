In this module, you used Azure AI Content Safety in Azure AI Foundry to test text and image moderation, groundedness detection, and Prompt Shields. You also saw how threshold settings change moderation outcomes and how the portal can export those settings as starter code for application integration.

As you continue building AI experiences, keep responsible AI practices at the forefront. Effective guardrails are layered: tune thresholds for your policy, validate retrieved outputs for groundedness, inspect inputs for prompt attacks, and secure your production integrations with strong authentication and secret management.

Azure AI integrates years of AI policy, research, and engineering expertise from Microsoft to help your teams build safe, secure, and reliable AI solutions from the start. Azure AI also provides enterprise controls for data privacy, compliance, and security on infrastructure that's built for AI at scale.
