The goal of this module was to help you manage volume capacity and performance on Azure NetApp Files.

You learned how to manage volume capacity and performance by:

- Monitoring the capacity of a volume
- Resizing a capacity pool or a volume
- Dynamically changing the service level of a volume
- Managing default and individual user and group quotas for a volume
- Managing storage with cool access

You also performed hands-on exercises to resize pools and volumes, update quota rules, and configure cool access.
