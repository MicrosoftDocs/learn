Your company wanted to move to Azure Active Directory (Azure AD) to manage secure access for its users. Users include many health industry customers, healthcare partners, and internal staff members. You were asked to investigate Azure AD for your company.

In this module, you saw the capabilities and features of Azure AD. You now have a greater awareness of what Azure AD is and how it differs from your on-premises instance of Active Directory. You also broadly understand the terminology used in Azure AD.

You now know the licensing costs and implications of using Azure AD, and you know the features for each type of license. You saw the best practices for a phased deployment of Azure AD into your enterprise. Also, you configured an Azure AD tenant from the Azure portal.
